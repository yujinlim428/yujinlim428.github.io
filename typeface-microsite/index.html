The name of typeface “Stadion” originates from the ancient Greek word stadion, which referred to both the stadium and the unit of a race. It reflects the scale and dynamism of modern sports arenas, capturing a powerful and intense atmosphere. “Stadion” uses a bold, slanted typeface inspired by the architecture and energy of players and audiences.
